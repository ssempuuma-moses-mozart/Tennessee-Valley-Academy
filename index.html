About Tennessee Valley

Tennessee Valley Academy
also known as Tennessee Valley Junior School

is a licensed primary school by the ministry of education (ME/P/11408) found in Katadde alongside Buwambo road, in Kasangati town council, Wakiso District. It is a non denominational school but respects the religion of an individual.

EXPECTATIONS:

We would like parents and learners to be aware of our expectations for academic performance. A good grade is a first grade and a satisfactory/average is second grade.

We are dedicated to discover and foster talents among our learners as a way of boosting their academic excellence and lighting up their future prospects.

Additional Information

Our School Schedules:

The school and class time table is available for both parents and learners. These timetables will be showing clearly the time for Music, Dance, P.E, Computer, Library, Swimming classes, etc.

School Uniforms:

Parents will purchase two sets of uniform for each learner from the school. Learners are expected to have a pairs of good black shoes and red stockings for schooling. Sportswear is also to be purchased from the school. Learners should put on sports shoes on the day of sports uniform. You can always arrange with our administrative assistant to have other sets of Uniform or sportswear. The school does not provide a swimming costume, so you can buy an appropriate costume for your son or daughter.

Book Bags:

The school will not provide school bags but may advise you the type of bag to buy. Learners are advised to be careful with their items by using these bags for safe custody.

Daily Classes:

The school will begin classes at exactly 7:30am - 4:00pm for primary section, and 7:45am – 1:00pm for nursery section from Monday to Friday.

Text Books:

The school will provide the learners with text books to use and a booklist for those interested in purchasing personal copies.

Meals:

Meals will be provided at school, they will include tea and a hot snack break. Lunch will also be provided. In case of an interest in packing a snack for your child let us know of it to check the healthy standards. The menu for lunch and supper (for boarding learners) will be provided to parents. It will be rotational but meals with pork will not be provided.

Academic Visitation day:

There will be an academic visitation ONCE every term communicated by the school. Parents are always encouraged check on their child’s note books and home work and boarding learners can be checked upon on every last Sunday of the month to monitor their progress. We will be having midterm reports for all learners issued on academic visitation day.

Pupils’ Leadership:

The school has a prefectorate body which is elected by pupils themselves; we encourage you to give your child confidence to stand for any available post.

Clubs:

We run different clubs to enable our learners build confidence, boost their brain power, improve their self esteem, instill great discipline, learn patience, teamwork, creativity, and build physical and emotional development.

Below are our clubs currently:

Music club (school choir and school band), Swimming, Badminton , Football, Net ball, writers club, art and craft, debate and quiz club(for primary) and storytelling club ( for nursery)

Religious affairs:

Children are given the opportunity to interact with leaders from their respective religious denominations during the religious affairs period every Wednesday. The Anglicans, Catholics, Seventh day Adventists and Muslims religious leaders are respectively considered.

Transport:

Transport will be provided to and from school for non boarding learners but at a cost. It is mandatory to sign a copy of rules and regulations governing the transport of learners. A route map will be drawn for convenience.

Care of property:

Parents, you can assist us with your child’s development by monitoring his or her personal property whenever she/he returns home. Learners will take better care of their property if they know that you will check on these matters and have appropriate consequences for improper behavior.

Learners are charged for any damage to school property and other consequences are provided following the school’s discipline policy.

Registration:

Registration will take place on arrival. A child is expected to have two recent taken photographs, a copy of report from his/her former school, and this admission letter. Please note that the school will not refund school fees after registration.
